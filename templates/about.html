About Our Smart Fruit Profiling System

Leveraging deep learning and computer vision techniques

Our Technology

The Smart Fruit Detection System harnesses YOLOv9 algorithms to revolutionize fruit analysis through deep learning and computer vision. It identifies fruit types, including apple, banana, mango, kiwi, and more, from simple image inputs, estimating weight and caloric content. The system also detects ripeness stages ripe, unripe, overripe, or rotten for fruits like grapes, oranges, and peaches, ensuring precise quality assessment. Utilizing a robust dataset from Roboflow, it supports over 30 fruit and vegetable types, such as bitter melon, cauliflower, and watermelon. Built with a front-end of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and a Python-based back-end on Flask within Google Colab, the system offers modules for home, registration, login, fruit detection, ripeness detection, and logout. This technology streamlines quality control and classification, delivering efficient, real-time solutions for agricultural and commercial applications.
